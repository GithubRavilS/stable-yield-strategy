When to Rebalance

Monitor these conditions for strategy updates

📉
Blended APY falls below your floor
Re-check HLP, Pendle and LP incentives vs. baseline scenario
📊
HLP vault parameters change
If HLP fee split, caps, or funding terms change materially, realign the HLP allocation to the target ~33% portfolio weight
📌
Stablecoin peg monitoring
Watch for unusual depeg or liquidity stress on issuers you use; rotate venues if quotes widen
🔄
Pendle maturity & pool rotation
When PT/YT mature or a better stable pool appears, redeploy so the Pendle allocation stays near the target ~33%
